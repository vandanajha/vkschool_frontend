WHY US...?


A child ’s mind is one of God ’s most wonderful creations and to nurture and develop it harmoniously is one of the most arduous but rewarding tasks . Mata Sukhdevi Public School , as a well established prestigious institution , is whole heartedly dedicated to the task of the harmonious development of the child's mind , body & spirit . Our cherished aim is to make the learning process child oriented , activity based and self motivated.
